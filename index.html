Cycling for health

To be fit and healthy you need to be physically active. Regular physical activity can help protect you from serious diseases such as obesity, heart disease, cancer, mental illness, diabetes and arthritis. Riding your bicycle regularly is one of the best ways to reduce your risk of health problems associated with a sedentary lifestyle.

Riding to work or the shops is one of the most time-efficient ways to combine regular exercise with your everyday routine. An estimated one billion people ride bicycles every day – for transport, recreation and sport.

About Us

Paddle On is a startup owned by young minds who believe in a change in our environment. Due to excess use of fossil fuel in recent years, we can see the change in cost of that media. Also, its effect on environment will be a problem in long run. We think our society should think about the future we are creating for the next generations to come. But excess use of petroleum or diesel vehicles are going to make it very hard to leave a good and healthy earth to our future generations.

Now-a-days we even book an app cab to travel close distances even when we have the time to travel the distance by foot or in a bicycle. Use of bicycle will be good for the environment as well as for the rider’s health. This small change will help our environment to heal, and reduce the excess use of fossil fuel. Also riding a bicycle will be much safer because of the newly created cycle lanes in every road around our country.
